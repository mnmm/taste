REVENUE SHARE. Subject to Vendor’s compliance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Taste will pay the Vendor 82% of the Total Order Cost placed with the Vendor via the Service (the “Vendor Cost”) for orders under 125 plate count and 79% for orders over 125 plate count.

PAYMENT TERMS. Taste will make payment to Vendor on net 30 from the Friday following the order, on every Friday (or next business day if the Friday is a banking holiday) for the aggregate Vendor Cost for the applicable orders fulfilled during the week preceding the 30 day period, i.e. on Friday February 13, Taste will pay Vendor for the week of January 5-9. Payments will be paid via direct deposit into Vendor provided bank account.

CONDITIONS & CANCELLATIONS. If an accepted order is cancelled by a User:

(a.)with more than 48 hours’ notice prior to the scheduled pickup time, no payment will be due to Vendor;

(b.)with less than 48h hours’ notice but more than 24 hours’ notice prior to the scheduled pickup time, Taste will pay Vendor 50% of the Vendor Cost that would be applicable to the respective order; or

(c.)with less than 24 hours’ notice prior to the scheduled pickup time, Taste will pay Vendor 100% of the Vendor Cost that would be applicable to the respective order.
